Bean Roll Ups

Description

A quick 10 minute dish for when you're in the mood for something spicy!

Ingredients

  • Bean dip
  • Flour tortilla
  • Shredded cheddar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Spread bean dip on a flour tortilla
  2. Top with shredded cheddar and roll up
  3. Brush with vegetable oil
  4. Bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees
  5. Serve with salsa
